Game Rules

  • Roll: Use WASD or Arrows to move your cube.
  • Rotate: Q and E spin the cube in place.
  • Red Face (Dash): Dashing lets you slide 2 tiles instantly. You must use this to kill Red Enemies.
  • Blue Face (Split): Schrodinger's Split divides you into two entangled cubes. Press SPACE again to instantly collapse back to the Parent (★).
  • Green Face (Tunnel): Quantum Tunneling allows you to step directly through a wall into the next space.
  • Cyan Enemies: These are entangled. You must hit one while you are also Split (Blue) to kill it.
